How to Use



The Air Swipe provides you the ease for you to present your presentations with your hand gestures



  • Next Slide: Place your hand in front of the camera with your four fingers extended (excluding the thumb) in an upright position. Then, swipe your hand from right to left to navigate to the next slide.

  • Previous Slide: Raise your index, middle, and ring fingers while holding your hand in front of the camera. Then, swipe your hand from right to left to go back to the previous slide.

  • Zoom In: Hold up your index and thumb fingers in front of the camera and move them apart to zoom in on a specific area of the slide.

  • Zoom Out: Bring your middle finger and index finger together while separating your thumb finger, then move them closer together in front of the camera to zoom out.
